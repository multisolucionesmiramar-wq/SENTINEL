Fisher-Rao Geometry as the Common Origin of Cosmological Bounces, Holographic Duality, and Geodesic Regularization
This paper proves that the Fisher-Rao metric — the only geometry on the space of probability distributions that is invariant under sufficient statistics (Čencov's 1982 uniqueness theorem) — induces an exact geometric structure on quantum systems isometric to Anti-de Sitter space. The same mathematical structure that governs black hole thermodynamics and cosmological singularities turns out to describe economic distributions. Sentinel applies this theorem to economic data, treating inequality, growth, and valuation as coordinates in a hyperbolic statistical manifold. The structural tension index (ITH_ext) measures geodesic distance from the sustainable attractor. The doomsday clock translates that distance into an intuitive signal.
